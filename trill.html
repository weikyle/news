Sacramento Kings

A lot of NBA GMs have asked me about me why I changed my middle name to Trill. Actually, the reason why I filed the paperwork to change my name was so that I could officially add my mother’s last name, Stein, to my own. My mom is my best friend, and I wanted to honor her. When I saw that I could change my middle name on the form, I added “Trill” because I never liked “Durmond.” Trill is a combination of true and real. It’s my philosophy.
